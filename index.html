cv


a degree in communication with hands on industry professional standards. gained presentation, video editing, audio editing, script/ news writing, sales and promotion skills. planned and operated a morning show two times a week and interned at rogers radio in ottawa as both a sound producer and later a morning show producer.

after finishing school i was hired in the first ever summer student program not in the newsroom. the fit was perfect and for three years i produced and participated in the kiss morning show. other notable achievements was producing a weekly best of show, entertainment series as well as podcast.

as one of a team of six, our job was to host 1900 passengers while floating through the oceans around australia and new zealand. this included developing and presenting trivia events to over 400 people, on stage improv performances and even hosting themed parties for passengers who were on board during the holidays. this job taught me the importance and skill of developing relationships and remembering faces.

bringing my ample audio editing experience and speed to the team i offer fast turn around and professional grade audio adjustments. the skill of editing audio or video is making it seem like you never did it. i am to make your voice sound naturally full and present. from mixing to mastering i am able to develop and coach podcasts to a final product.

with this locally owned business i did everything from facebook advertising to youtube video creation. you can see one of the first videos i made for the business in my portfolio that grossed over 15,000 views. working alongside management and front desk staff i was able to bring their online presence to a new level. most notably, post engagement rose more than 600% while i was publishing content for them.
